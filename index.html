MISSION: From DyingTo Living

Population of Jangipur Subdivision of Murshidabad is about 2 million. Jangipur Sub Division is one of the most backward subdivisions of India. There is no source of commercial activities for the people of the area except Biri making (smoking sticks made by leave and tobacco) from which revenue is very poor. Almost every family of the area is engaged in this activity. Its like cottage industry. Every member of the house is involved in biri making to earn bare minimum livelihood. Unfortunately, in the process, engaged population become victim of Tuberculosis like diseases, due to handling of tobacco. Its like accepting death for livelihood.

A process is essential to initiate to diversify the workforce from Biri to other activities. A humble effort has been started aiming above families keeping above activities in place.
